The Constitution for Ex-convicts
Living Document
The Founding Fathers intended to make the Constitution into a living, changeable document. The ability of future legislation to change the Constitution and add amendments allowed for adaption of it for new technologies and problems the country may face.
Why It Matters
The Constitution is the foundation from which all laws and government in America is created. Every part of modern-day America is an extension and result of the ideas set forth by the Founding Fathers. By learning the Constitution, Americans can get a better understanding of how and why they can improve the world around them.
What We Teach
The goal of this course is to both encourage that disproportionately affected groups get the education on the U.S. legal system that they deserve and promote action and engagement in the government. We focus on the specifics of the Constitution and how they apply to the lives of ex-convicts.
